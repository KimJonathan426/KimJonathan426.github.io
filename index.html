About Me

I am an innovator, a creator, and a problem-solver; I am a software engineer and a biological engineer.

I was introduced to programming when I was completing over 132 credit hours studying bioengineering. I used languages like MATLAB or Python primarily as a tool to analyze, model, and solve problems surrounding biological systems. After graduating, I went into the bio-pharmaceutical industry and felt that something was missing. I did not realize how much an impact coding had in my life until I was in an environment deprived of it.

I wanted to expand my knowledge and discover the potential of coding past biology. Thats when I discoverd App Academy and was able to quickly shift gears. In only 16 weeks, I invested over 1000 hours of work to learn new stacks and create full stack applications. I flourish in fast-paced and intellectually stimulating environments which are portrayed through my projects below.
